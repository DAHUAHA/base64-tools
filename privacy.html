Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

base64.tools ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website www.zisaac.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We do not require account registration. The text you encode or decode is processed in your browser and is never sent to our servers for the core encoding/decoding feature.

1.2 Conversion Logs

When you use the API features, we store a lightweight record of each conversion in Upstash Redis (hosted in the EU). Each log contains:

  • The first 50 characters of your input text
  • The first 50 characters of the output
  • Conversion mode (encode or decode)
  • A anonymized IP address (first 3 segments only)
  • Timestamp

These logs are retained for up to 100 entries per database and are automatically pruned.

1.3 Automatically Collected Data

Like most websites, we collect server access logs including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. These are used solely for security, performance monitoring, and abuse prevention.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain the service
  • Detect and prevent abuse (e.g., rate limiting)
  • Improve service performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. We may use essential cookies for security purposes only.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Vercel — website hosting and deployment
  • Upstash — Redis database for conversion logs
  • Cloudflare — DNS management and DDoS protection

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, Content Security Policy headers, and rate limiting. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not store personally identifiable information and no account is required, there is no data to delete or export. If you have concerns, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

contact@zisaac.com

9. Google AdSense

If we introduce advertising in the future via Google AdSense or similar networks, third-party ad servers may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
