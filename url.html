What is URL Encoding?

URL encoding converts characters into a format that can be transmitted over the Internet. Special characters (like spaces, Chinese characters, symbols) are replaced with "%" followed by two hexadecimal digits. This ensures URLs remain valid and parseable across all systems and browsers.
